Admissions

Admission requirements vary by department, but generally include a completed application form, official high school transcripts (minimum GPA 3.0), letter of intent, and standardized test scores if applicable. Specific programs may require separate portfolios or entrance essays.

The Fall semester application period opens on January 15th and closes on May 1st. The Spring semester application period opens on September 1st and closes on November 15th. Early decision applicants have alternate timelines.

Yes, students applying for Science and Engineering departments must take a specialized math and physics placement exam. Business and Design students are evaluated heavily on essays and portfolios instead.

Absolutely. OmniVault welcomes students globally. International applicants must provide proof of English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS) and translated academic credentials vetted by approved review boards.

Courses & Enrollment

You can enroll directly via the Course Details page on this portal. Simply browse our Catalog, select your desired module, and click the 'Enroll' button to begin your registration. Ensure you have your Student ID ready.

Yes, our interdisciplinary framework allows you to select up to two elective courses per semester from external departments, provided you satisfy the prerequisite logic for those modules.

Undergraduate students may take a maximum of 18 credits (typically 6 courses) per semester. Postgraduate students are capped at 12 credits to ensure dedicated research allocation.

Withdrawals can be requested via the Admin Dashboard up until the 4th week of the semester without penalty. Late withdrawals will be recorded as 'W' on your permanent transcript.

Fees & Scholarships

Tuition varies strictly by the enrolled department and level. On average, undergraduate credit hours cost $300, while specialized post-graduate lab credits cost $450. Please check with accounting for specific bracket queries.

Yes. OmniVault offers Merit-Based, Need-Based, and specialized Departmental Fellowships. Funding pools reopen every Spring semester. The application can be submitted simultaneously with your admission packet.

We securely accept all major structural payment methods including Wire Transfers, Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and certified local bank drafts. Installment plans are available upon explicit inquiry.
